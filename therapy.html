My Approach

Curious, active,
and genuinely present

Being curious and asking why is a defining quality. Surface answers were never enough — complex interactions and connections are what capture attention. That's why people and their lives are so endlessly interesting.

Therapy needs to be practical. Change needs meaningful steps that both of us understand. We start with simply raising awareness — that's enough to begin sorting facts from perceptions. Patterns emerge as we uncover truths and determine objective reality.

You'll see them in yourself, in others, and in your relationships. This provides a direction toward what you can change — even when the situation and players stay the same. This internal sense of control is more accurate and more powerful. It takes away hopelessness and instills relief.

"When you're feeling stuck, there's always another way. We'll figure it out."

01

Raise Awareness

We begin by slowing down enough to notice what's actually happening — in your thoughts, your patterns, and your relationships.

02

Look Back with Adult Eyes

Together, we take a look at old stories and their beliefs to see what holds up under scrutiny — and what does not. Not everything you were taught about yourself is true.

03

Identify What Can Change

Even when situations and other people don't change, your relationship to them can. We find where your real leverage is.

04

Find Your Center

You are the ultimate observer of your own life. You'll find your center and feel empowered to decide — rather than waiting for change to happen to you.
